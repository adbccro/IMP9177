Receiving Inspection · SOP-PM-021
Rev 4 · Effective [on signoff]
Page 1 of 8

1. Purpose

This SOP establishes the procedure for receiving inspection of incoming raw materials, components, and finished goods at the 3H Pharmaceuticals LLC facility. It implements the requirements of 21 CFR Part 111 Subpart E and FSMA §117.130 for supply-chain traceability.

2. Scope

This procedure applies to all materials received at the facility loading dock, with the exception of office supplies and non-product materials non-product materials, R&D-only Amazon-sourced ingredients (per DEC-035), and office supplies.

3. Responsibilities

The Receiving Clerk QA-trained Receiving Operator shall perform initial inspection on all incoming shipments. The QA Approver shall review and release each lot before storage.

4. Procedure

4.1 Pre-arrival — verify the PO number, expected vendor, and lot manifest in the SharePoint Receiving list.

4.2 Physical inspection — visually check packaging integrity, count, and labeling against the PO.

4.3 CoA verification (NEW) — For each lot received, confirm the Certificate of Analysis is on file in the Vendor Documents library before tagging "Received." If no CoA is present, place lot on Quarantine and notify the QA Approver. CoA must match the lot identifier on the package.
