Variant 08

Delegate signature — modal

Triggered from the "Delegate signature" card on the DCO detail. Authorizes a named alternate to sign on your behalf for a bounded scope and time. The delegation itself is a Part 11 sig event — both creation and revocation generate hashed audit rows. Open-ended delegations are rejected.
